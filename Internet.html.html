How ICT FOR PEACE?

 

I strongly believe that ICTs can and have already demonstrated the potential to:

  1. Reduce loss of life.

  2. Provide acess to critical, real-time information, crucial in timely and appropriate decision-making in crisis situations.

  3. Enable of the Government of recipient countries to make well-informed and decision regarding allocation of resources and means through which victims can give feedback regarding and promised and received;

  4. Create institutional memory of crisis management operations which are are often characterised by a rapid turnover of staff.

  5. Improve the safety and security of all personnel in crisis areas and

  6. Improve situational awarness and create opportunities for early-warning on threats.
